About

Ivo Mengerink

I’m Ivo Mengerink, engineer and founder of IMeTech Engineering. With a background in mechatronics and hands-on experience across multiple engineering disciplines, I help businesses turn technical ideas into working products.

My passion lies in making technology understandable, practical and effective. Whether it’s product development, embedded systems, PCB design, mechanical engineering or custom software — I bring it all together under one roof.

Based in Aalten, in the heart of the Achterhoek, I work closely with companies that value direct communication and a hands-on approach.

“Making technology understandable, practical and effective for every business.”
