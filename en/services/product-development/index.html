FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do you guide the full journey from idea to prototype?

Yes. I guide product development from concept and requirements to a working prototype, bringing mechanics, electronics, embedded systems and software together in one trajectory. You have a single point of contact instead of separate specialists.

Do you work with external suppliers and production partners?

Yes. I coordinate where needed with production partners, assembly companies and external specialists. Think PCB fabrication, CNC milling or 3D printing. You keep one line of communication, even when multiple parties are involved.

What does an initial product development call cost?

The discovery call is free and non-binding. After that you receive a clear quote based on scope, timeline and deliverables per phase. This way you know upfront what to expect.

What types of products do you develop?

From industrial control systems and sensors to consumer products and IoT devices. As long as it is technically challenging and combines mechanics, electronics or embedded software, it fits well with my expertise.

Do you work remotely on product development?

Yes, with regular updates and clear deliverables per phase. Based in Aalten in the Achterhoek, I work with clients across the Netherlands; on-site sessions are possible when physical tests or measurements are needed.
