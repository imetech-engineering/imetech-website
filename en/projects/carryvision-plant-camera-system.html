CarryVision – Plant Camera System

Timelapse and monitoring for nurseries, built with ESP32 cameras and a Raspberry Pi.

March 2026
ESP32 Raspberry Pi IoT Embedded Product development
In collaboration with CarryFlor

CarryVision is a complete plant camera system commissioned by CarryFlor. It tracks plant growth over time through automated captures and timelapse video — all accessible from a dedicated dashboard, on-site and over the internet.

The system comprises two custom camera modules in 3D-printed enclosures, each built around ESP32-CAM. The cameras shoot on fixed intervals and send images wirelessly over WiFi to a Raspberry Pi 5 that acts as local server and dashboard host.

The web dashboard lets users view live feeds, browse photo history, and generate timelapse clips for any selected range in a few clicks. Multi-user accounts, per-camera schedules, and image rotation support flexible mounting.

IMeTech covered the full lifecycle: electronics and firmware, printed housings, server-side software, and end-user documentation. The system was delivered plug-and-play — ready after connecting to the network.
