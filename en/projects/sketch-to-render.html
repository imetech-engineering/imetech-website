Project Description

The Sketch to Render project demonstrates the complete development process of product development, from initial conceptual sketch to photorealistic 3D visualization. For this client, I fully developed their product: from rough sketch with main dimensions and materials, via technical CAD model with precise measurements, to professional 3D renders with realistic lighting and material settings.

The process always starts with a rough sketch in which the core of the idea is captured. No details, no perfection, just the concept. From there, it is built up in SolidWorks CAD: exact dimensions, material properties, mounting points and technical details. The design must be both technically feasible and visually appealing, with every dimension and connection carefully considered.

The real transformation happens in the rendering phase. Here the concept comes to life with proper lighting, realistic material settings and attention to reflections and shadows. Suddenly you no longer see just a technical model, but the actual product as it could come into production. This visualization makes the difference between concept understanding and concrete product experience.

What stands out in this process is how visualization can change the perception of a product. Where clients may still have doubts or understand the concept at the first sketch, they often become immediately enthusiastic at the render. The difference between "I understand the concept" and "I want this product" is made by this professional visualization, which shows the product in a way that comes close to reality.
