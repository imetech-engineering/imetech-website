Project

Soldering Station

Dual-purpose thermal rework system with precision closed-loop temperature management.

February 2024
PCB Design Thermal Management HMI Design Embedded Control

The Multifunctional Soldering Station integrates a high-reliability soldering iron and a hot air rework tool into a single ergonomic workspace. Both channels are independently managed by a custom-designed control system, featuring a high-contrast digital interface for temperature and airflow calibration.

The core electronics consist of a complex custom PCB developed in KiCad, running specialized firmware that provides real-time thermal feedback and safety shutdowns. The case design, created in SolidWorks, utilizes a professional mix of high-heat-resistant 3D-printed internals and natural wood accents for a premium workbench look.

Optimized for precision PCB assembly and high-level prototype repair, this station provides industrial-grade performance in a compact, locally-developed package.
